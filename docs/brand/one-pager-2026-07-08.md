# One-pager — print collateral (2026-07-08)

**What:** a single-page leave-behind Conner can print to PDF and attach to founder emails or hand across a desk. Template: `public/brand/one-pager.html` — open it in a browser, Print → Save as PDF, Letter, default margins ("Background graphics" ON so the cream ground and clay accents print).

**Content rules applied:**
- Every claim mirrors a ratified customer surface (home / how-it-works / pricing). No customer counts, no testimonials, no logos, no awards — zero customers means zero proof theater (Truth Wave).
- Model vendor invisible. The AI is "the fleet"; the providers are named only in the /privacy subprocessor list, which the one-pager does not reproduce.
- The dollar figure is the ROI calculator's own labeled default ($4,300/mo at 10 hrs × $100/hr), stated WITH its assumptions — the same treatment as the home-page exhibit.
- Pricing states the tier shape and trial/guarantee facts from `lib/billing/facts.ts` values as of 2026-07-08. If billing policy changes, regenerate this page — it is a snapshot, not a live surface.
- The founder note is deliberately spare (name, model, the dogfooding fact already public in the site FAQ). When the founder-credibility surface (PR #375) merges, lift its ratified bio line into the note and re-export.

**Update cadence:** re-export after any pricing, guarantee, or claims change. The HTML is hand-kept — it duplicates copy on purpose (print collateral must not break when the site refactors), so a quarterly diff against the live pages is the hygiene loop.
