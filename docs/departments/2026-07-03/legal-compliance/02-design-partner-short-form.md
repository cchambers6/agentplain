## DESIGN PARTNER AGREEMENT

This Design Partner Agreement ("Agreement") is between **[ENTITY], d/b/a
agentplain** ("agentplain," "we") and **[PARTNER LEGAL NAME]** ("Partner," "you"),
effective on the date of last signature ("Effective Date").

**1. The pilot.** We will provide Partner access to the agentplain service — a
fleet of AI assistants that drafts business communications and reports for
Partner's review — for a pilot term of **three (3) months** from the Effective
Date ("Pilot"), at **no charge**. The service **drafts only**: every output lands
in Partner's approval queue as a pending draft, and nothing is sent, filed, or
committed to any third party except by Partner, from Partner's own systems.

**2. What Partner agrees to do.** During the Pilot, Partner will (a) join a
weekly 30-minute feedback call; (b) connect the business tools reasonably needed
for the pilot workflows; and (c) **review every draft before sending it** — the
service assists Partner's judgment and does not replace it.

**3. What agentplain agrees to do.** We provide the service, dedicated founder
time during the Pilot, and early access to new features. We will not send
communications on Partner's behalf, and we will not use Partner's data to train
any AI model.

**4. Data.** Partner's data remains Partner's. Data read from Partner's connected
tools is processed to produce drafts and is not copied into long-term storage;
the assistant's working memory for Partner's workspace persists for the life of
the account and is deleted on account closure, per the Privacy Policy at
agentplain.com/privacy. Partner may disconnect any tool or close the account at
any time.

**5. Intellectual property.** We own the agentplain service and everything that
makes it work. Partner owns Partner's data and all approved, sent communications.
Partner grants us a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use suggestions and
feedback given during the Pilot to improve the service, with no obligation and no
compensation. Neither party acquires the other's trademarks or brand.

**6. Publicity and case study.** The parties intend to co-author a case study.
Neither party will publicly name the other — including logos, testimonials, or
case-study content — **without that party's prior written approval of the
specific use**. Partner's approval may be withheld or withdrawn for any reason.

**7. Not professional advice.** Drafts may relate to regulated subjects (for
example, real-estate communications). The service's compliance checks are
advisory only. Nothing the service produces is legal, tax, brokerage, or other
professional advice, and Partner is solely responsible for the content Partner
approves and sends.

**8. Term and termination.** Either party may terminate this Agreement for any
reason on **fourteen (14) days' written notice**, or immediately on the other
party's material breach. On termination we deactivate the workspace and delete
Partner's account data per the Privacy Policy. Sections 5–7 and 9–11 survive.

**9. Warranty disclaimer.** The Pilot service is provided **"AS IS,"** without
warranties of any kind, express or implied. It is an early-stage product provided
free of charge for evaluation and co-development.

**10. Liability cap.** Except for a party's willful misconduct, or a breach of
Section 6 (publicity), neither party is liable to the other for indirect,
incidental, consequential, or punitive damages, and each party's total liability
under this Agreement is capped at **one hundred U.S. dollars ($100)**. **[COUNSEL:
confirm cap structure and carve-outs are enforceable for a zero-fee pilot under
Georgia law.]**

**11. After the pilot; general.** At Pilot end, Partner may subscribe at
then-current published pricing or walk away — no obligation, no auto-conversion,
and no card is taken under this Agreement. This Agreement plus the Terms of
Service and Privacy Policy at agentplain.com are the entire agreement; **this
Agreement controls over them during the Pilot** where they conflict. Georgia law
governs; venue **[COUNSEL: confirm venue/dispute clause]**. Signatures may be
electronic.

## Notes for counsel (not part of the agreement)

1. **Why $100 and not fees-paid:** fees paid are $0; a fees-paid cap could be read
   as zero consideration for the cap itself. Flag if a different structure is
   safer.
2. **Why publicity is a cap carve-out:** the case study/testimonial is the entire
   commercial value we receive; a partner-side breach of our name is likewise the
   partner's chief risk. Symmetric and mutual.
3. **Section 7 is load-bearing.** The whole liability posture is draft-only +
   human approval (the no-outbound architecture). If any clause gets strengthened,
   it is this one.
4. **What is deliberately absent:** non-compete (unenforceable/unnecessary),
   exclusivity, SLA (none is staffed — /security copy is being softened to match,
   PR #354), indemnities (disproportionate for a free pilot — flag if one is
   needed), and any compliance warranty (the sentinel is advisory; Section 7 says
   so).
5. **Consistency check run 2026-07-03:** free-3-months, weekly-30-min call,
   testimonial + reference ask, case-study-with-approval all match the published
   one-pager (`docs/marketing/design-partner-outreach/real-estate/design-partner-program-one-pager.md`,
   PR #353). If the offer changes, both documents change in the same PR.
