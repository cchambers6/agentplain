# What marketing must stop — with restart triggers

The planning session's diagnosis stands: marketing's bottleneck is not asset quality, it is distribution, proof, and measurement. The department's failure mode is producing more inventory against zero sends. Each stop below names what is frozen, why, and the specific trigger that thaws it. "Stop" means no fleet cycles and no founder attention, not deletion; everything stays on disk.

## 1. STOP: ad-creative production, entirely

25 concepts, 51 outbound creative files, and paste-ready platform copy exist against a paid gate that is 0-for-4 and now explicitly HELD until the first partner signs (kill list, PR #354). Every additional variant depreciates unused. This includes "just one more hook" edits to existing concepts.
**Restart:** first signed design partner AND measurement live. Then production starts from the existing 25, not from new brainstorming.

## 2. STOP: all non-real-estate content and asset polishing

CPA, law, PM, and general pieces, landing-page deepening, FAQ expansion, narrative refreshes: closed. The kill list closes those GTM fronts until 2 RE pilots are live; content for a closed lane is inventory for a warehouse we are not renting. Existing pages stay live and truthful; they just get no new investment.
**Restart:** 2 RE pilots live (per-vertical order then follows the sales plan, CPA first).

## 3. STOP: new positioning, narrative, and strategy documents

The deep-dive already says the assets are good and the motion is missing. The canon is locked; the comparison story is written; the voice is codified and gated. Any marketing session that produces a strategy doc instead of a published piece or a wired instrument this window is misallocated, including "Pass 2" refinements of the deep-dive itself.
**Restart:** the planning cadence's own triggers (first partner signed, or a load-bearing rule change), or the monthly floor.

## 4. STOP: photography commissioning and visual-system expansion

The five golden-hour briefs stay parked (HOLD in the kill list). The Heritage system plus existing components carry the five pieces. No new tokens, no new editorial utilities, no mascot variants.
**Restart:** first signed partner (the ratified trigger), at which point the first shoot is the partner, not stock scenery, which is a strictly better photo anyway.

## 5. STOP: social-channel buildout beyond handle reservation

Reserving handles so the brand resolves on search stays done; posting calendars, follower programs, and platform-native content formats do not start. One exception rides the existing motion at zero cost: each published piece may be posted once to the reserved accounts as a plain link with one gate-clean line. No engagement farming, no cadence promises.
**Restart:** content cadence sustained 4 weeks AND a human or agent owner named for replies, because an unanswered social surface reads worse than none.

## 6. STOP: generating unbatched founder decisions

The kaizen retro named it: marketing generates decisions faster than the one decision-maker clears them, and the queue machinery sat empty while decisions scattered across documents. Marketing now files decisions only through the Conner queue with a recommended default and an explicit hold-state fallback, batched to the weekly review. No new ad-hoc "flagged for Conner" lines in documents, this series included: this window marketing adds zero new decisions beyond the four already queued in `04`.
**Restart:** n/a; this is permanent hygiene.

## 7. STOP: measurement sophistication beyond the five instruments

The scorecard is sends, replies, calls booked, pieces published, source-known %. No dashboards, no attribution modeling, no cohort tooling until there is volume to model. The deep-dive's rule holds: no number without a decision attached.
**Restart:** paid test live (which brings cost-per-trial and needs real attribution), or 100+ weekly sessions making first-touch UTM reads statistically meaningful.

---

**What explicitly does not stop:** the five-piece pipeline, the measurement week-1-2 scope, the outbound collateral in `03`, gate discipline (Truth Wave, voice-gate, brand-gate never pause), and the weekly citation/scoreboard reads. That is the whole department until the first partner signs.
