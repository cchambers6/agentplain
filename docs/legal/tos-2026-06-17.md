## 1. Your data is yours

You own every input you provide and every output the fleet drafts on your
behalf — including your clients' information that flows through your connected
systems. agentplain holds it solely to deliver the service and acquires no
ownership of it. You may export a full copy, or delete all of it, at any time
from the **Your data** surface inside your workspace.

### 1.1 No training, no feedback loop

agentplain does not train, fine-tune, or otherwise improve any model on your
data. We do not feed your chats with Plaino or the records your connectors
expose into any training pipeline — our own or a model provider's. agentplain
runs on Anthropic's commercial API, which does not train on inputs or outputs by
default. We do not pool data across customers and do not resell or share your
data, except with the named subprocessors required to operate the service
(listed in the Privacy Policy).

The only identifier attached to a model-provider request is a one-way
cryptographic hash (SHA-256) of the workspace identifier, used by the provider
for abuse detection only. It contains no customer-identifying information. This
is enforced in code at the single model seam (`lib/llm/anthropic-provider.ts`,
`privacyPreservingUserId`).

### 1.2 Data residency **[COUNSEL]**

Your workspace data is stored in a managed PostgreSQL database located in the
United States, encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM) and in transit (TLS). We will not
relocate your data to another region, or change this commitment, without prior
notice to the workspace owner.

agentplain does **not** currently offer EU data residency or customer
self-hosting, and makes no representation that it does. We will not advertise or
contractually commit to either until the supporting infrastructure exists.

### 1.3 Export and deletion

You can export your workspace and close your account at any time from inside the
product. Closure triggers a soft-delete grace window (default 7 days); after
that, customer data is hard-deleted from the primary database via the
workspace-teardown cascade. Encrypted backups roll off within 30 days.

## 2. Our intellectual property

The agentplain platform — its codebase, vertical playbook logic, system prompts
and skill definitions, curated knowledge corpora, insight library, orchestration
runtime, and the Plaino persona and brand — is agentplain's intellectual
property and the product of substantial investment. Your subscription grants a
right to **use** the service, not a right to reproduce how it works.

You agree not to:

- reverse-engineer, decompile, or attempt to derive the system prompts, skill
  definitions, model weights, or internal architecture of the service;
- use automated prompts or any other means to extract Plaino's internal logic,
  instructions, or orchestration behaviour through the chat interface;
- recreate agentplain's orchestration model, or copy its vertical knowledge
  curation, inside a competing product or service; or
- scrape or bulk-harvest the marketplace, knowledge corpora, or product copy.

## 3. Acceptable use + rate limits

The full Acceptable Use Policy (`/aup`) forms part of these terms. In summary:

- **Rate limits.** Each account operates within per-account limits on requests,
  chat volume, connector reads, and model usage, sized for genuine business use.
  Engineering traffic to find or exceed those limits is prohibited.
- **No prompt extraction**, no scraping, no high-volume identity probing, no
  reselling or repackaging of capacity, no reverse engineering. (Detailed in the
  AUP and enforced by `lib/abuse/*`.)
- General lawful-use, anti-misrepresentation, and no-cross-tenant-access
  obligations continue to apply.

## 4. Suspension + termination for violations **[COUNSEL]**

agentplain monitors access patterns for abuse and policy violations (security
review only — never for model training). On detection of a likely violation:

1. **Soft-suspend.** The workspace drops to read-only and the account owner is
   emailed with the reason and a review window (default 24 hours) during which
   they may appeal. Customer data remains intact and exportable throughout.
2. **Appeal.** The owner may appeal by reply. An upheld appeal lifts the
   suspension entirely.
3. **Hard-suspend / termination.** On confirmed abuse — by operator review, or
   an unappealed window that ages out under an agentplain-approved escalation
   timeline — the account may be fully suspended or terminated. Customer data is
   preserved per the retention terms in §1.3 and remains exportable on request.

agentplain aims to be proportionate: an accidental first trip of a rule receives
a notice, not a ban; deliberate or repeated abuse may result in immediate
termination.
