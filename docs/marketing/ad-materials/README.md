## Calibration (the three judgment calls)

**Strongest single headline across the pack:**
> **"It drafts. You sign. Nothing leaves without you."**

It's the whole moat in seven words — done-for-you *and* the human-in-the-loop control an
auto-send tool can't promise — and it travels across every vertical. The realty runner-up,
**"Drafts ready by 6:30am,"** is the best concrete-scene headline for paid search.

**Best-fit vertical for first paid spend: realty.**
It is the only one of the five on the **Regular** self-serve tier ($99/seat) — the lowest
friction from ad click → free trial without a sales call. It also carries the highest ROI
multiple (~26×), the most concrete creative (the 9:14pm counter-offer drafted by 6:30am),
and the deepest high-intent search demand. **Home-services** is the paid #2 (Meta + Google,
owners convert on ROI, not on LinkedIn). **Law** and **finance** are Max / quote-based and
sales-led — route those to founder outreach, not cold paid, until there's a sales motion to
catch the lead.

**DTC channel to start this week: founder-led LinkedIn + cold email to a tight realty
design-partner list.** Zero media budget, highest-trust first touch, and it seeds the
reference customers the rest of the pack needs. Pair it with a small Google Search test on
realty pain keywords as the first paid dollar.

integrations claimed: email (Gmail/Outlook) + Google Calendar + QuickBooks (+ realty

AI" — drafts land in your queue on a cadence (prod LLM key is paused; degraded-mode is
today's reality). We are the done-for-you service layer **on** Claude, never a competitor to
it. Banned-words list enforced. ROI figures cite `lib/verticals/<v>/content.ts`.
