## Budget & bidding

- **Start:** $40–60/day per active metro. Trades search intent is bursty (storm events spike roofing/restoration); cap by metro, not nationally, so a storm in one market doesn't drain the whole budget.
- **Bidding:** open on **Manual CPC** or **Maximize Clicks** with a CPC ceiling for the first ~30 conversions to seed clean data, then move to **Maximize Conversions / tCPA**. Conversion = `Start free trial` (`/app/sign-up`) and `Talk to a service partner` (talk-to-Plaino) — both are real events per ground truth; weight the trial start higher.
- **Storm-season note:** roofing demand is weather-driven. Hold ~20% budget in reserve to lean into a metro the week a storm hits — that's when "every lead got a drafted reply by lunch" lands hardest.
- **Audience layering:** apply in-market (business services / B2B software) + observation on company-owner signals; this is the cheapest defense against homeowner leakage beyond negatives.

## Quality-score considerations

- **Relevance:** mirror the ad-group theme verbatim in headlines (estimate-follow-up group → "Estimates Go Cold. Win Back."). One pain theme per ad group; don't blend supplement + call-triage in one group.
- **Landing page:** point to the home-services vertical page, not the generic homepage — the headline promise ("drafts; you approve") must be the first thing above the fold, matching the ad. Defer all price/schedule language to the product; the page must not imply auto-send.
- **Expected CTR:** the no-auto-text / TCPA line is a genuine differentiator no competitor can echo — it carries CTR honestly. Lead with it in at least one pinned asset.
- **Honesty gate:** every asset above defers price + scheduling to the operator and never claims the fleet sends. No banned words used. Verify against the five quick tests before any asset goes live.
