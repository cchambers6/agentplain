## Budget & bidding notes

- **Daily budget:** start $40–60/day. Law CPCs run high ($15–40+ on broad legal terms);
  the long-tail pain groups above keep effective CPC lower and intent higher.
- **Bidding:** start **Manual CPC** (or Maximize Conversions with a low cap) for the first
  ~3 weeks to control cost while data accrues. Conversion = "Talk to a service partner"
  form submit + trial start, with the scoping call weighted higher.
- **Schedule:** weight business hours, Tue–Thu; firm decision-makers search at the desk.
- Lean ad copy and the strongest sitelink toward the **scoping call**, not self-serve —
  law buyers want a person before a card.

## Quality-score considerations

- Landing page must restate the **draft-then-approve** control above the fold and name the
  **live** integrations (email, calendar) honestly — no Clio/MyCase logos.
- Keyword → ad → LP message match: a "conflict screen" search lands on a page that shows
  the conflict-screen scene, not a generic AI pitch.
- Avoid superlatives the page can't back; law buyers (and Google's policy review on legal
  terms) punish unsubstantiated claims. State the ROI with its firm-size qualifier.
