## Targeting

**Job titles (primary):** RIA Principal · Founding Advisor · Managing Partner · President (Wealth Management) · Owner (Financial Advisory) · Practice Manager · Director of Operations · Chief Compliance Officer (small RIA).

**Seniority:** Owner / Partner / CXO / Director.

**Industry:** Financial Services · Investment Management · Financial Planning.

**Company size:** 2–50 employees (independent + small-mid RIAs — where the principal still writes the letters). Layer a separate campaign for 51–200 toward Max/multi-office.

**Groups / interests:** NAPFA, XY Planning Network, FPA, CFP/CFA holders, Kitces/Nerd's Eye View readers.

**Exclusions:** retail-investor titles, students, job seekers, wirehouse W-2 advisors at the largest broker-dealers (poor fit vs. independent RIAs), existing customers.

> Run InMail/Conversation ads to the 2–50 segment (principal still does the admin) and Sponsored Posts broader for awareness. Optimize to **scoping-call bookings**, not impressions — this is a Max/quote sale.
