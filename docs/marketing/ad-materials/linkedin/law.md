## Targeting

- **Job titles:** Managing Partner · Partner · Solo Attorney · Owner (Legal Services) ·
  Firm Administrator · Director of Operations (law firm) · Of Counsel (decision-influencing).
- **Industry:** Legal Services (+ Law Practice).
- **Company size:** 1–10 and 11–50 employees (solo, small, and small-mid firms — the
  ROI math is cited at 3-attorney and scales to 25-attorney).
- **Seniority:** Owner, Partner, Director, VP.
- **Exclude:** students, paralegal/legal-assistant-only seniority (non-buyers), current
  customers, competitors.
- **Format priority:** Conversation ad and InMail for owners/managing partners; Sponsored
  post for reach + retargeting. Bid: cost-cap, optimize for the scoping-call conversion.
