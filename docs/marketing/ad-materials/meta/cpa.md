## Image-direction brief

**Mood:** heritage, grounded, lived-in. A real accounting firm in busy season.

- **Hero still:** an actual partner's desk at dusk — a desk lamp on, a stack of client folders, a coffee gone cold, a laptop showing a calm "approvals queue" list (rows reading "drafted — pending your approval"). Warm, not frantic. The story is *the late night that didn't have to happen.*
- **Human:** a credentialed CPA (40s–60s), reading glasses, reviewing on screen, hand on the mouse about to approve — the human-in-control beat, shown not stated.
- **Palette:** warm prairie/heritage tones, paper, lamp-glow amber. Plaino may appear small as the grounded brand mark on the laptop screen, never as a cartoon "AI helper."
- **Hard NO:** stock handshakes, glowing-brain/robot imagery, futuristic blue HUDs, confetti, stock "diverse team high-fiving," any visual implying the machine sends on its own.
- **On-image text (if any):** one short line max — "The fleet drafts. You sign." Keep under 20% text coverage.

## Audience targeting

- **Lookalikes:** 1–3% LAL from (a) free-trial signups, (b) `Talk to a service partner` bookings, (c) CPA landing-page visitors (180-day). Seed once pixel volume allows; until then run interest stacks.
- **Interest stacks (layer AND across 2 of these):**
  - AICPA · American Institute of CPAs · Journal of Accountancy
  - QuickBooks ProAdvisor · Intuit ProConnect · QuickBooks Online (interest, not a live-integration claim in copy)
  - Small accounting firm / public accounting / tax preparation
  - Firm-owner signals: "small business owners" + job titles Partner / Managing Partner / Owner at accounting firms
- **Geo/size:** US, English; bias toward independent and small-firm metros over Big-4 corridors.
- **Exclude:** current customers, employees/job-seekers (interest: "CPA exam," "accounting jobs," "careers"), students, and existing free-trial signups (suppress from prospecting; move to nurture).
