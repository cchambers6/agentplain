# Meta Ads — Law (law firms)

**Vertical:** `law` · **Tier:** Max (quote-based); Partner floor · **Trial:** 14-day
**CTA bias:** "Talk to a service partner" / "Learn more" over self-serve trial.

> Channel framing: **Meta is lower-intent for law.** Decision-makers don't search Facebook
> or Instagram for matter-management help; they're scrolling off-hours. Treat Meta as
> awareness + retargeting, not direct-response capture. Goal of the click is to start a
> scoping conversation, not close a card. Keep the promise small and the tone formal — law
> buyers distrust hype more than most.

> Honesty spine: the fleet drafts; an attorney approves every client-facing draft. Nothing
> is sent or filed by the fleet; legal conclusions are `{{attorney: …}}` merge fields. Live
> integrations = email + Google Calendar only.

## Image-direction brief

- **Subject:** a real law-firm setting — a wall of bound reporters and a clean desk, a
  partner's office at dusk, a stack of matter folders with a pen resting on the top one.
  Heritage, restrained, lived-in. Warm, low, even light.
- **Hard NO:** gavels, scales-of-justice clip art, stock men in suits shaking hands, robots,
  glowing "AI" brains, neon circuitry, courtroom drama, a judge's bench.
- **Treatment:** muted, document-forward, calm. The image should read "a careful firm,"
  not "a tech product." Plaino, if present, sits small and quiet in the corner — never the
  hero of a law creative.
- **Text overlay:** one short line max, set in the heritage type. No badges, no starbursts.

---

## Carousel (3 slides)

1. **Intake comes in.** A new matter lands. The conflict screen runs in seconds — an
   adverse-party check, deterministic, every time.
2. **The fleet drafts.** Intake acknowledgement, conflict notice, status update — drafted
   into your queue. Legal conclusions stay merge fields for the attorney.
3. **You approve.** An attorney reviews and approves every client-facing draft. Nothing is
   sent or filed without your name. → *Talk to a service partner.*

---

## Story format (vertical, 15s)

- **0–3s:** Static — a stack of matter folders, text: "New matter. Conflict check."
- **3–8s:** "An afternoon of screening —" → cut to a clean queue → "— runs in seconds."
- **8–12s:** "The fleet drafts. An attorney approves every word."
- **12–15s:** agentplain mark. "Done-for-you AI for law firms." CTA: *Talk to a service partner.*
- No motion-graphics flash; slow cross-dissolves, quiet.

---

## Audience targeting

- **Lookalikes:** 1–3% lookalike off any scoping-call/trial-start list once seeded; off
  website visitors to the law landing page meanwhile.
- **Interest stacks (broad, low-intent — expect to refine):** Law practice management ·
  Legal technology · American Bar Association · Above the Law · Solo & small firm interests ·
  Job title interests: Managing Partner, Attorney, Law Firm Administrator.
- **Retargeting (primary value on Meta):** law-LP visitors, video 50%+ viewers, scoping-call
  page abandoners.
- **Exclude:** law students, bar-exam interest, paralegals (job-seeker overlap), existing
  customers, consumer "find a lawyer" audiences.
- **Frame:** budget Meta as a supporting/retargeting line, not the primary acquisition
  channel for law — LinkedIn and search carry intent; Meta carries reminder and reach.
