# Meta Ads — Real Estate (realty)

Vertical: `real-estate` · Tier: Regular ($199 → $99/seat) · Trial: 7-day, card at signup,
first month free, 14-day money-back.

The fleet **drafts and proposes; the broker approves and sends.** No auto-send, no "instant
AI," no glowing-robot imagery. Live integrations: email + Google Calendar + QuickBooks +
DocuSign + Google Drive (realty day-one). Lowercase `agentplain`; capitalize `Plaino`.

## Image-direction brief

The look is heritage and grounded — paper, ink, morning light, a real working desk in a real
brokerage. Palette is the brand's: warm paper/cream, deep ink, forest green.

**Show:**
- A desk at dawn: a laptop open to an approvals queue, a coffee cup, a printed listing sheet,
  a pen resting on it — the broker's own world, before the day starts.
- A single document on screen with one figure circled in pen (the "change one number" beat).
- Plaino (the 8-bit robot dog on the plain) only as a small, calm brand mark in the corner —
  never the hero, never anthropomorphized into a "smart assistant."

**Never show:**
- Stock handshakes, sold-sign high-fives, suited agents grinning at a tablet.
- Glowing robots, neon AI brains, holographic UI, blue-gradient "tech" sheen.
- Anything implying the software is sending or posting on its own (no paper-airplane "sent"
  animations, no auto-fill magic).

## Audience targeting

- **Lookalike sources:** 1–3% lookalikes built from (a) trial signups (`/app/sign-up`
  conversions), (b) `Talk to a service partner` leads, (c) any uploaded broker-owner contact
  list from founder outreach. Seed quality > size — keep the source list realty-only.
- **Interest stacks:** real estate brokerage · National Association of Realtors · real estate
  team leader / managing broker · transaction coordination · DocuSign · real estate CRM
  (as interest, not a live-integration claim). Layer "small business owner" to bias toward
  owner-operators, not individual agents.
- **Exclude lists:** existing customers + active trials (suppress via customer-list match) ·
  real-estate license-exam / "get your real estate license" interests (wrong intent) ·
  large-enterprise brokerage job titles that route to Max/sales, not self-serve trial ·
  anyone who clicked but bounced sub-5s on the LP (retarget separately with the compliance
  angle, not the same ad).
