## Voiceover-direction notes

- **Register:** warm, plain, owner-to-owner — a heritage local-business partner, not a corporate announcer. Conversational pace, no upsell lilt.
- **Pacing:** let the numbers breathe — "23," "19," "35 minutes," "$42,000" are the payload; don't rush them.
- **Hard NO:** hype reads, no "instant," "magic," "transform," "supercharge"; no excited exclamation tone; no robotic AI-narrator voice.
- **Tone of the scene:** the late night that *didn't* have to happen — relief, not adrenaline.
- **Never imply** the machine sends or files. Every script ends on the human signing.
