## VO-direction notes

- **Voice:** a fellow shop owner, not a narrator. Warm, plain, unhurried. Think someone who's run a crew for 20 years telling you what finally fixed his back-office mess — across the counter, not from a stage.
- **Pace:** leave air. No fast-read announcer cadence, no hype lift at the end.
- **Banned in read:** any line that implies the system sends, texts, or books on its own. Never "instant," "automatic," "watch the magic." The control line — *you approve and send* — is non-negotiable in the 15s and 30s.
- **No price beyond** the published $199/seat and "first month free." Estimate amounts, discounts, and schedule never appear — those defer to the operator.
- **Music:** sparse or none. If used, grounded and steady — no EDM build-drop.
- **End always** on the human's control plus first-month-free, never on a countdown or scarcity.
