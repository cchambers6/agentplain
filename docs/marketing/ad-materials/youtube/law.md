# YouTube Ads — Law (law firms)

**Vertical:** `law` · **Tier:** Max (quote-based); Partner floor · **Trial:** 14-day
**CTA bias:** "Talk to a service partner."

> Honesty spine: the fleet drafts; an attorney approves every client-facing draft. Nothing
> is sent or filed by the fleet; legal conclusions are `{{attorney: …}}` merge fields.
> Live integrations = email + Google Calendar only.

> **VO direction (applies to all cuts):** calm, precise, credible — a senior peer, not a
> pitchman. Measured pace, full stops, no upspeak, no music swell. NOT a legal-thriller
> trailer, no dramatic stings, no "in a world…" gravel. Think a careful partner explaining
> something true. Picture is restrained: a real firm library, a clean desk, matter folders.
> No gavels, no robots, no glowing brains.
