## Voiceover direction

- Warm, plain, owner-to-owner — a fellow broker who's been there, not a corporate announcer
  and not a hype-y product voice.
- Measured pace. Let the 9:14pm / 6:30am beats land with a small pause; the scene does the work.
- No rising "and that's not all!" energy, no smile-in-the-voice salesmanship. Confident and
  calm, like scenario library's "across the counter" test.
- Never imply the software acts on its own — every line keeps "you approve / you send" intact.
- No banned words in ad-libs: no "transform," "seamless," "magic," "supercharge."
