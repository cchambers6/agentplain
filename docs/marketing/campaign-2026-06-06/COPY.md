## Testing notes

- **First test matrix per concept:** lock the winning headline first (3 headlines × B1 × CTA1),
  then test body, then CTA. Don't test all 27 combinations at once — insufficient volume per cell.
- **Sound-off discipline:** every video variant ships with burned-in captions; LinkedIn + Meta
  Reels are majority sound-off.
- **Claim hygiene (every variant):** never imply the fleet sends/files/dispatches autonomously;
  ROI figures must match the cited source; "first month free," never "pilot"; on general/umbrella
  placements, name the vertical breadth (page-one-verticals rule, `feedback_everything_tells_a_story.md`).
- **Persona-channel pairing:** C2 long-form → LinkedIn/P2 primary; C1 → Meta+Search/P1; C5 →
  Meta storm-geo+YouTube/P3 (see CHANNELS.md).
