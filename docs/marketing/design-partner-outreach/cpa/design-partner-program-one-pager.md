## The problem

By mid-March, the firm's hours have shifted from preparing returns to chasing the
documents needed to prepare them. The same K-1 reminder, the same 1099 follow-up,
written by hand, tracked in someone's head. It's repetitive, it's manual, and it scales
straight into overtime.

And the work carries real exposure. Under Circular 230 and IRC §6694, a preparer
position that understates a client's liability is a $1,000–$5,000 per-return penalty.
That risk belongs to a credentialed professional — never to a black-box tool.

## What agentplain does

agentplain is a done-for-you service, not a tool you have to drive. We install it,
configure it for your firm, and run a monthly review.

- **Month-end close.** The fleet drafts status updates and missing-document chases,
  tracked per client, into a queue.
- **Finance weekly pulse.** It reads your QuickBooks AR aging and drafts a short
  weekly read of where things stand.
- **Onboarding letters.** It drafts new-client engagement and onboarding correspondence
  for your review.

**The honesty spine.** The fleet drafts and proposes — that's all. It does not send,
file, pay, or state a tax position. Every item sits pending until a credentialed person
at your firm approves it. What you approve goes out from your own systems. The approval
gate is the product, not a footnote.
