# First-Contact Outreach — general (SMB on-ramp)

Two variants: **cold** (no prior relationship) and **warm** (referral or prior contact).
Each is 100–150 words. Plaino voice is fine here. Sign as Conner.

The ask is small: a 15-minute call. The pitch is one line: the fleet drafts the admin work; you approve and send.

---

## Variant A — COLD

**Subject line options (pick one):**
- the admin that lives in your head
- six hours a week back

**Body:**

Hi {{first_name}},

You run {{business_name}}, which means you also run the inbox, the scheduling, the follow-ups nobody else picks up, and the dozen small things that only live in your head. That work doesn't show up on any job description — it just eats your week.

Here's what we do. agentplain installs a fleet of capable AI partners inside your business, connects your email and calendar, and drafts that routine admin into an approvals queue. You read it, you approve it, you send it. Nothing leaves without your name on it — the fleet never sends, files, or commits anything on its own.

We're opening a small design-partner cohort: free for three months, with direct founder time during the pilot.

Worth 15 minutes to see if it fits?

Conner Chambers, founder, agentplain
