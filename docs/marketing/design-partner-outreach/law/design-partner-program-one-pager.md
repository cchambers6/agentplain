# agentplain for Law Firms — Design-Partner Program

*Intelligence rooted in reality.*

agentplain is a done-for-you AI service for law firms. A fleet of AI partners drafts the
routine, time-draining work — adverse-party conflict screening, client status updates,
after-hours intake acknowledgements — and lands each draft in an approvals queue. An
attorney approves every client-facing piece before it leaves the firm. The system does
not send, file, or state a legal conclusion. It drafts; you decide.

---

## The problem

Three kinds of work eat billable hours at a small firm:

- **Intake conflict screening.** Every new matter needs an adverse-party check before
  anyone can talk to the client. It has to happen and no one bills it.
- **Status-update drafting.** Clients expect to be kept current. Writing those updates
  between deadlines is constant, low-value, and easy to let slip.
- **After-hours intake.** Inquiries arrive nights and weekends and sit until someone
  reaches them — a slow first response on the matters most likely to convert.

And the floor under all of it: ABA Model Rule 1.6 (confidentiality) and Rule 7.1 (no
misleading client-facing claims). Get either wrong and the exposure runs to malpractice
and disbarment. Any tool near client work has to respect that.

---

## What agentplain does

We install a fleet of AI partners inside your firm, connect your tools, and run a monthly
review. Live workflows today:

- **Intake conflict screen** — a deterministic adverse-party check that compares a new
  party against your matter list and drafts an internal notice. The legal conclusion is a
  merge field an attorney fills.
- **Document-review acceleration** — drafts that speed first-pass review for an attorney's
  eyes.
- **Status-update drafting** — client-update drafts that land in your queue for approval.

The honesty spine is the whole design: **the fleet drafts; an attorney approves; nothing
sends itself.** Every client-facing draft is pending until a licensed person signs off.
The system never sends, files, advises, or states a legal conclusion. Live integrations
today are email and calendar — case-management systems are roadmap, and we'll say so
plainly.

A note on the numbers: any ROI we discuss is operator-modeled, not customer-attested.
We have zero clients today — that is precisely why this program exists.
