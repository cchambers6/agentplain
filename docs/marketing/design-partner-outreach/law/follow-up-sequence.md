## Email 2 — T+7 days · the Rule 1.6 / 7.1 exposure downside

**Subject:** where the exposure actually sits

Hi {{first_name}},

A fair question about any tool near client work: what's the downside if it's wrong?

It's real. ABA Model Rule 1.6 governs confidentiality; Rule 7.1 bars misleading
client-facing claims. Exposure runs to malpractice and disbarment. That is precisely
why agentplain is built so the system never sends, never files, and never states a
legal conclusion on its own. It drafts; an attorney approves every client-facing
piece before it leaves the firm.

The safeguard isn't a promise the AI behaves. It's that a licensed person signs off
on everything, every time.
