## The problem

A broker-owner's week fills with coordination, not closings. Inbound leads need a
fast, personal first touch — and the ones that wait overnight go cold. Commission
invoices need chasing. Transactions generate a steady drip of email that has to be
answered now, not later. None of it is the work you got your license for, and all
of it competes with the clients in front of you.

## What agentplain does

We install a fleet of capable AI partners, connect your tools, and run a monthly
review. The fleet:

- **Triages inbound leads** — scores each one and drafts a first-touch reply in
  your voice, into your approvals queue.
- **Chases commission invoices** — reads overdue invoices off QuickBooks and drafts
  an escalating chase for each.
- **Summarizes overnight activity** and drafts your monthly report.

The honesty spine: every draft lands in a queue as PENDING. **You** approve and
send, from your own inbox and CRM. The fleet never sends, files, or commits
anything on its own. A compliance sentinel scans each draft against the HUD
enumerated-phrase list and advises before you approve — it flags, it never blocks.
That human gate is the whole design.
