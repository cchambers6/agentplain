Vertical: `real-estate` · Tier: Regular ($199 → $99/seat) · Trial: 7-day, card at signup,
first month free, 14-day money-back.

5 touches over 14 days. The fleet **drafts and proposes; the broker approves and sends.**
Live integrations: email + Google Calendar + QuickBooks + DocuSign + Google Drive. No FUB /
MLS / kvCORE claims. Subject lines: lowercase-leaning, ≤8 words, no exclamation, no emoji.

---

## Personalization tokens

- `{{FirstName}}` — recipient's first name. Greeting + warmth.
- `{{Brokerage}}` — brokerage name. Shows the note is to them, not a blast.
- `{{City}}` — their market. Grounds the email in their world.
- `{{AgentCount}}` — number of agents/team size. Scales the ROI framing.
- `{{SenderName}}` — who's writing (Conner or a service partner). Signature.
- `{{CalLink}}` — booking link for the 15-min chat. Only used from touch 3 on.
