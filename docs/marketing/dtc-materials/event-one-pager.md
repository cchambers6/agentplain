## The pain

You didn't open your business to spend the night chasing documents, replying to the same
inbound questions, and writing the follow-ups that go cold while you sleep. But that work
doesn't stop — and the AI tools that were supposed to help just sit in a tab, because nobody
has time to set them up, connect them, and check what they spit out.

## What Plaino does

agentplain installs a fleet of capable AI partners inside your business, built for your line
of work, and runs it for you. Plaino — your named partner — reads what's already there and
drafts the routine work into a queue you control:

- **Drafts your first-touch replies and follow-ups** — the inbound lead, the estimate nudge,
  the intake acknowledgement — warm, on-brand, ready to send.
- **Chases what's outstanding** — overdue invoices and missing month-end documents, pulled
  straight from your books (QuickBooks) and inbox.
- **Writes the summaries you skip** — the overnight recap, the monthly report — so you start
  the day with a queue, not a blank page.
- **Checks itself against your rules** — a compliance review reads every draft against your
  industry's requirements and flags anything risky *before* you approve it.

**It drafts; you approve and send — from your own inbox, in your own name.** The fleet never
sends, files, pays, or books anything on its own. Connected today: email (Gmail / Outlook),
calendar, and QuickBooks. Real estate adds DocuSign and Drive.
