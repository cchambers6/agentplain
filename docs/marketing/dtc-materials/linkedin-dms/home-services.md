# LinkedIn DMs — home services (roofing · HVAC · plumbing · electrical · GC)

**Vertical:** `home-services` · **Tier:** Partner ($299 → $199/seat) · **Trial:** 7-day, card at signup, first month free, 14-day money-back.
**Voice law:** `docs/marketing/CREATIVE_PACK_GROUND_TRUTH.md` + `docs/marketing/brand-voice-scenario-library.md`.
**Recipient:** owner / GM / office manager of a trades shop. Plain, curious, soft ask first — owner to owner, never a pitch-bomb.
**Honesty spine:** the fleet drafts; the owner approves and sends. Live integrations = email + Google Calendar + QuickBooks. Never imply auto-send. No price beyond $199/seat.

> Channel reality: many trades owners are light LinkedIn users. Keep it human and short; lead with a question, not a pitch.

**Tokens:** `{{FirstName}}`, `{{CompanyName}}`, `{{Trade}}`, `{{City}}` — same rules as the cold-email file. Drop any token that's missing rather than guess.

## Variant 1 — the estimate that went quiet

**Step 1 — connection note (≤300 chars):**
> Hi {{FirstName}} — I work with trades shops on the office side, the estimate follow-ups and after-hours calls that pile up. Curious how {{CompanyName}} handles follow-up. Mind connecting? (≈185)

**Step 2 — opener after connect:**
> Thanks for connecting, {{FirstName}}. Honest question: how many estimates went quiet last month because nobody chased them at the right time? We're agentplain — the fleet drafts those follow-ups and lands them in your queue, you approve and send. Nothing goes out on its own. Worth a look?

**Step 3 — follow-up if no reply (3–4 days later):**
> No worries if follow-up's already handled, {{FirstName}}. If it's not, the fleet drafts the nudge at the right stage and hands cold ones back to your rep for a call. First month's free if you ever want to see your queue fill. Either way, glad to be connected.

## Notes

- One ask per message. Connection note = a curious question, not a pitch. Opener = the value + soft ask. Follow-up = release, never a third push.
- Always name the control (you approve and send) and the no-auto-text fact where it fits — it's the trust anchor.
- Allowed numbers only: $199/seat, $50,000+/yr, first month free. Never quote an estimate, promise a slot, or imply the fleet sends.
- No banned words. Across-the-counter test before every send.
