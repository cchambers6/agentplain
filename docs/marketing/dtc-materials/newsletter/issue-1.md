You signed up to hear how this works. Here's the whole argument, plainly.

**The problem isn't that AI can't help local businesses. It's that local businesses can't use it.**

The tools are real. But to get value out of them, someone has to pick the right one, write the prompts, connect it to your inbox and your books, check the output, and keep all of that working as things change. That someone is you — the owner — and you're already doing the work the AI was supposed to take off your plate. So the AI sits in a tab, unused, while the routine work keeps eating your mornings.

That gap is the whole reason we exist.

**What we do.**

agentplain is the done-for-you service layer on Claude. We're not another tool for you to learn. We're the service that installs and runs it for you:

1. We install a fleet of capable AI partners, already built for your line of work — real estate, accounting, law, the trades, financial advice.
2. We connect your real tools. Today that means your email (Gmail or Outlook), your calendar, and QuickBooks. For real-estate firms, DocuSign and Drive too. We connect what's live and we tell you plainly what isn't yet.
3. The fleet reads what's already there and **drafts** the routine work — the first-touch reply, the month-end document chase, the overnight summary. Each draft lands in your approvals queue.
4. You open the queue, read the draft, change what you want, and send it from your own system. Or you don't — every item sits PENDING until you decide.
5. Once a month, we sit down with you and tune it.

**The part that matters most: nothing leaves without your name on it.**

The fleet drafts and proposes. It does not send, file, pay, post, or book anything on its own. That's not a limitation we're apologizing for — it's the design. You're the licensed, accountable professional. The work goes out under your judgment, in your voice, on your say-so. A compliance check reads each draft against your industry's rules and flags anything risky before you approve — it advises; it never sends.

This is the difference between "AI that runs your business" (which no serious owner wants) and "AI that drafts the busywork so you can run your business" (which is what you actually need).

**Why "on Claude."**

Claude is the engine. We're the service that makes it usable — configured for your vertical, connected to your tools, run for you for a flat fee. You get the capability without becoming the person who has to operate it. That's the easy way to actually use Claude.
