## The night this is built for

March 17. Twenty-three clients still missing documents to close their books. The old version of that evening is one partner, alone, writing twenty-three different reminder emails — each citing the specific items that client still owes — long after everyone else has gone home.

## What Plaino does for your firm

We install a fleet of capable AI partners inside your firm, connect your tools, and run a monthly review. The fleet **drafts** the routine work into an approvals queue. **You approve and send** — from your own system. Nothing files on its own.

- **Month-end close.** Drafts per-client status notes and missing-document chases — each reminder citing the exact items still outstanding, and skipping clients already on extension.
- **The document chase.** The recurring busy-season grind, drafted for you, ready for a credentialed person to review and send.
- **Finance pulse.** Reads your QuickBooks — AR aging, open invoices — and drafts a short weekly summary into your queue.
- **Onboarding letters.** Drafted from your template, ready for your sign-off.
- **Compliance sentinel.** Reads each draft against Circular 230 and a PII scan, and **advises** — it flags risk before you sign. It never edits, blocks, or sends.
