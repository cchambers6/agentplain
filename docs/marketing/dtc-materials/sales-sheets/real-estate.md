## What Plaino does for your brokerage

Plaino is your named AI partner. The fleet drafts the routine work overnight and lands it in
your approvals queue. Every item sits PENDING until you decide. **The fleet drafts and
proposes; you approve and send — from your own inbox, your own e-sign, your own books.**

- **Lead triage.** Scores every inbound buyer lead and drafts a first-touch reply — warm,
  plain, asking the right qualifying questions.
- **Commission-invoice chasing.** Reads your open commission invoices from QuickBooks and
  drafts a chase for each one, escalating in tone as they age.
- **Overnight transaction summary.** Reads the day's deal activity and writes a clean recap
  into your queue by morning.
- **Monthly report draft.** Pulls the month together so your update is written, not started
  from scratch.
- **Fair-Housing scan.** A compliance sentinel checks every listing draft against the HUD
  enumerated-phrase list and flags risky language. It advises — it never posts.

**Connected day one:** Outlook · Gmail · Google Calendar · QuickBooks · Google Drive · DocuSign.
