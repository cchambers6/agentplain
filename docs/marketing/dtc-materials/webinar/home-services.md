## 30-minute agenda (timed)

- **0:00–3:00 — The work that piles up after the work.** The estimate that went quiet nine days ago. The storm Monday the phone won't quit. The supplement left on the table. Why jobs walk on follow-up, not price.
- **3:00–8:00 — What agentplain is.** Done-for-you, not a tool you drive. We install the fleet, connect your email, calendar, and QuickBooks, and run a monthly review. Plaino, your named partner.
- **8:00–14:00 — Live: the estimate follow-up.** Watch a drafted nudge land in the approvals queue at the right stage. See a cold one handed back to the rep for a call. The owner approves and sends — nothing goes out on its own.
- **14:00–19:00 — Live: storm Monday.** A morning of call volume; every lead gets a drafted reply in the queue by lunch. Where the TCPA line matters: we never auto-text, so a non-consented blast can't go out by machine.
- **19:00–23:00 — Live: the insurance supplement.** A short-paid claim; the fleet matches the missing line items and drafts the rebuttal for review. The $50,000+/yr math, plainly.
- **23:00–25:00 — Pricing and getting set up.** Partner at $199/seat, first month free, month-to-month. How install and the monthly review actually work.
- **25:00–30:00 — Your questions.** Open floor.
