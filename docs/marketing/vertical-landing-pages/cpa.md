## What Plaino does not do

We are precise about this, because your license depends on it.

- **It never states a tax position.** Every quantitative or tax claim is left as a merge field for the credentialed preparer to fill. The fleet drafts the letter; you supply the position.
- **It never files anything.** No return, no extension, no e-file is submitted by the fleet.
- **It never moves money.** No invoice is sent, no payment is taken, no AR is touched without an approval that carries your firm's name.
- **It never auto-sends.** Every output is a pending approval. A person on your team approves and sends.
