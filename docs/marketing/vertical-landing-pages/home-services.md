## What Plaino does NOT do

We'd rather you trust this than oversell it.

- **Plaino never quotes a price.** Numbers are yours. Every draft leaves the price and the schedule as a blank for you to fill in.
- **Plaino never books the job or commits a date.** Scheduling stays with you.
- **Plaino never auto-sends.** Nothing leaves your shop without you approving it first. Every output is a pending draft with your name on it — you approve, your system sends.
- **Plaino never moves money.** No charges, no transfers, no invoicing on its own say-so.
- **Your field-service software sync is still setting up.** AccuLynx, JobNimbus, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, and Jobber aren't connected yet — connect your field-service software as it comes online. Today the estimate follow-up works from your estimate records and your email, which is enough to stop the bleed while the rest gets wired.

That last point matters: a homeowner text falls under the TCPA, where a non-consented message runs $500 each — up to $1,500 if it's willful, with no cap. That's exactly why agentplain drafts and you send against your own consent record. A machine-sent blast is the one promise an auto-dialer can't make. We won't make it either, on purpose.
