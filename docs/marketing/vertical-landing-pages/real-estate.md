## The part of the job nobody tells you about

**"A lead came in at 9 last night and I saw it at 10 this morning. By then they'd already toured with someone else."**
After-hours buyer inquiries are the ones that go cold fastest, and they always land when you are off the clock.

**"My CRM is a junk drawer. Duplicates, dead contacts, half-filled records. I clean it up once a quarter if I'm lucky."**
The hygiene drift an unstaffed CRM accumulates between clean-ups is quiet until it costs you a closing.

**"One wrong sentence in a listing description and I'm looking at a fair-housing complaint. I read everything twice and I'm still nervous."**

**"I'll get to the production report next month."**
You have said that every month this year. It never gets built by hand, so it never gets built.

That is 8 to 12 hours of your week. As the broker-owner, those are your most expensive hours.

## What Plaino does not do

We are honest about the line, because the line is the point.

- **It does not auto-send.** Every reply, every nudge, every draft waits in your approvals queue until you approve it. Nothing leaves with your name on it unless you put it there.
- **It does not move money or make commitments.** No transfers, no signatures on your behalf, no promises to a client.
- **It does not make the fair-housing call for you.** The scanner flags and suggests. Your broker-of-record reads and signs. We pre-check; we do not certify.
- **It does not replace your broker-of-record, and it is not a substitute for legal counsel.**
- **MLS auto-pull is not live yet.** Pulling listings and production directly from your MLS comes online once those connections are built (Q3 2026, below).
