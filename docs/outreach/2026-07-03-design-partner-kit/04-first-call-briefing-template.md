## One closing frame

{{Write the single next step you want, with a date. Examples: "If design-partner fit: state the terms plainly — 3 months free, weekly call, case study you approve word by word — and book the kickoff before we hang up." **Never end on "I'll follow up sometime."** A yes is a calendar entry; a not-yet is a date in the CRM; a no gets the one learning question: "what would have had to be true for this to be a yes?"
