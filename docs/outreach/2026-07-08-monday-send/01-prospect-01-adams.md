## Why this prospect

A 45-year independent brokerage where the founder is still the president and the firm still carries his name is the purest form of the ICP: durable, relationship-run, and owner-operated. A shop that has stayed boutique for four decades didn't do it by adding staff layers — the coordination work (doc chasing, late lead replies, month-end invoicing) almost certainly still routes through the principal. And a firm this durable buys on trust and plain dealing, which is exactly the register of the design-partner offer: honest terms, founder access, no proof yet and says so.

## Custom hook
