## Why this prospect

Their own site describes the thing most independent shops never build: a full-time coordination staff supporting every agent. That is the strongest possible signal that the firm values (and pays for) exactly the drafting-and-chasing work we install — and it reframes the pitch from "replace effort" to "make your coordinators senior": they review drafts instead of typing them. A firm proud of its service layer is also the most likely to *understand* a drafting layer immediately. Risk worth naming: a staffed shop feels less pain than a drowning solo broker, so the sale is efficiency and capacity, not rescue.
