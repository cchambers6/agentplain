Their anti-franchise stance ("no corporate bureaucracy, no franchise roadblocks") also mirrors our independent, no-lock-in story. One honest caveat from the shortlist stands: they are marketing-sophisticated, so the "tech-forward automation shop" disqualifier is a live risk. Screen it on the call (discovery question 5: what tools run the business), not before. If they've already built their own stack, disqualify warmly and ask who else in Cherokee County belongs on the list — brokers who teach know everyone.
