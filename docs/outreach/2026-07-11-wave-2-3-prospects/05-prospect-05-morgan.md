## Why this prospect

Two partners, no staff, 166 closed homes on the archive — the broker IS the business.
Everything Park Realty does that isn't a client conversation is a partner doing admin:
every doc chase, every 1031 deadline reminder, every invoice. Investor services and
1031 work are deadline-driven and unforgiving, which makes drafted reminders concrete
value rather than nice-to-have. This is also the honest stress test of our smallest-shop
economics: if design-partner feedback says the drafting layer doesn't pay at two
people, we learn where the floor really is — from operators articulate enough to say so.
