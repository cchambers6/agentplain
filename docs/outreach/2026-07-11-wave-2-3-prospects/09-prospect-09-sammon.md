## Why this prospect

He ran a top-ranked RE/MAX franchise for nearly twenty years and walked away from the
brand to build an independent — his own bio says he did it "to make joining the real
estate field an easier transition for new agents." That's a broker who already did the
math on what franchise overhead buys and chose to own his stack instead. It also means
he gave up the franchise's back-office scaffolding on purpose, and at 24 agents with a
training-first mission, the onboarding and coordination load lands on him. The
independence story mirrors ours: no lock-in, tools he controls, month to month.
