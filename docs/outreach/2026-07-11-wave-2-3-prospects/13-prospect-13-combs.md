## Why this prospect

Henry County is one of the fastest-growing corridors on the south side, and Grove Side
is the opposite of the shops chasing that growth: a three-person family firm whose
tagline is about roots. The brand alignment is almost literal — "intelligence rooted in
reality" pitching "WE HELP YOU Put Down Roots!" At three people there is no ops layer
at all; every follow-up, doc chase, and invoice is family time. A 25-year licensee who
is also a third-generation local buys on plain dealing and stays for decades — small
revenue, but the retention profile and the Henry County word-of-mouth are the prize.
