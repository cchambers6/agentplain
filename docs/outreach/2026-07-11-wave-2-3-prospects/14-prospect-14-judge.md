## Why this prospect

A founder who put solar panels on the office roof and her own art collection on its
walls runs the firm as an expression of values — and values-led independents are the
best fit for a service whose pitch is "your people stay the ones who hit send." The
ops math is also the heaviest in Savannah: 15 people spanning sales, commercial, and
500 managed rentals means a permanent river of owner statements, maintenance
follow-ups, and doc chases beside the sales pipeline. She's a 20-year operator with
published civic commitments competing for the same hours.
