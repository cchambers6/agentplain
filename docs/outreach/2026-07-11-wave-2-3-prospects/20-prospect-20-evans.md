## Why this prospect

She markets herself on being operationally present — the homepage's contrast is that
while other brokers are out, "Rose is in office." That is the pitch's mirror image: a
broker whose brand is presence pays for it in her own hours, seven days a week, across
a seven-person team working two states. The dual GA/SC footprint doubles the paperwork
surfaces (two commissions, two doc conventions). Nobody on this list will recognize the
"who does the chasing? I do" question faster, because she's built her marketing on the
answer.
