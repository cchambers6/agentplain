## Why this prospect

Gena Knox built a consumer-products company into 10,000 retail doors before she ever
hung a license — she thinks in systems and operating leverage, not in agent-speak. That
makes her the rare prospect who will evaluate a drafting layer the way an operator
evaluates a supply chain. And the timing is real: a 7-person shop that just opened a
second market (Lake Oconee) has doubled its coordination surface — two markets' worth
of listings, leads, and closings on the same small roster. Luxury positioning raises
the stakes on response quality, which is an argument for drafts a human approves, not
against them.
