1. **Approvals ledger for the week:** ✅ real screen, every item with status. Rejections would show with reasons (`decisionReason` is captured); this week had none.
2. **The saved-time number with its honesty label:** ⚠️ showable, but the number is **wrong-by-omission** — the counter renders only the inbox chain's minutes because lead triage writes no ledger rows (P0-3). The runbook's script ("does that match your gut?") makes this *worse* in the broken state: Sarah's gut says the lead work was the valuable part, and the counter doesn't show it. In the dry-run Conner names it straight: "the counter's missing the lead work — our counting gap, being fixed, and I'd rather undercount than guess." Survivable exactly once. The Truth Wave posture (undercount + say so) is the only playable move, and it's the move the docs already prescribe.
3. **One draft before/after her edit:** ✅ the edited-before-approve rows make the side-by-side real.
4. **Connections tab:** ✅ everything green, honestly renderable.

What's NOT shown (internal dashboards, spend, vendor names, projections): the discipline holds — nothing in the product forces a vendor name onto the screen-share. ✅
