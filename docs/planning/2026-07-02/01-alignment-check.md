## Summary

No load-bearing rule is being actively violated in a way that would embarrass the business in
front of a customer or counsel — the fleet's discipline on outbound, BYO, vendor invisibility,
and truthful claims in *new* work is genuinely good. The two findings that matter:

1. **The one CONTRADICTING row is the operating system, not the product:** decision flow to
   Conner is broken by omission, and every other row's "correction" column ultimately routes
   through it.
2. **Truth Wave drift is now a money bug, not a copy bug** — the saved-time undercount can
   trigger wrongful refunds. That upgrades it from brand hygiene to a P0 product fix.
