## Copy-discipline notes (for marketing)

- Lead deltas with the **liability/compliance** angle in regulated verticals (title, RIA, insurance, mortgage, recruiting), and with **done-for-you service** against the platforms and horizontals.
- Do **not** lead with "you stay in control" as if unique — Claude for Small Business, Roots/Bevaya, and Capacity all ship approval gates. Lead with *why our gate is smarter* (the draft already respects your industry's rules).
- Never claim a vertical we can't back with a counsel-reviewed corpus + reference (per the honesty bar). Real estate is provable today; phrase the others as "built for, compliance review in progress" where true.
- Home-services deltas must be scoped to "back-office augmentation" — do not imply we answer phones or book jobs.
