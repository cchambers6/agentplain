## 1. Stanley Dean — SDA CPA Group, P.C. (Dunwoody)

- **Role:** Founder, CPA — 35+ years in Atlanta; BBA + Master of Accountancy, UGA; founded the firm 2003 (`sdacpa.com/about-sda-cpa`)
- **Size:** team page names 9 people (`sdacpa.com/about-sda-cpa`) — in band
- **Practice mix:** accounting/bookkeeping, tax, payroll, small-business advisory; **no audit visible**. In Sept 2016 the firm publicly dropped non-business-owner 1040 work to focus solely on small businesses (`sdacpa.com`, `sdacpa.com/about-sda-cpa`)
- **Stack:** "QuickBooks Online and third-party apps," year-round engagement model; client portal "Double (formerly Keeper)" (`sdacpa.com`) — QBO-centric, portal-comfortable: two strong ICP signals
- **Community:** GSCPA member; Professional Association of Small Business Accountants; UGA Bulldog 100 six times, most recently 2025; "My Dunwoody 2025 Best CPA" (`sdacpa.com/about-sda-cpa`, `sdacpa.com`)
- **Why this prospect:** a firm that fired an entire client class to protect its focus understands the pitch in one sentence — buying back partner attention. Year-round small-business clients mean monthly books and doc chases all year, not just in season, which fits a summer/fall pilot perfectly.
- **Custom hook:** the 2016 decision to drop 1040-only clients — a deliberate-focus story from their own site.
- **Pre-send check:** confirm the about page still names Stanley Dean as active (not retired); confirm the 2025 Bulldog 100 claim is still current framing.
- **Disqualifier risk:** low. Second office in Cumming. Family-run marketing (Kelly Dean).
