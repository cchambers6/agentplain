## Field 5: quantified outcome + methodology (CPA-adapted, honest)

**Three numbers, all ledger- or thread-derived, none estimated:**

1. **Hours reclaimed per week** — per-action time credits from the saved-time ledger, conservative calibration table (`lib/guarantee/` as of capture date), counted **only for actions the fleet actually completed** in the partner's workspace between two named dates. Expected honest band: **2–5 hrs/week off-season** — deliberately below the season-peak research anchor, because the ledger counts completed fleet actions, not the whole coordination burden, and because an off-season pilot measures off-season work. If the pilot spans a season ramp, the seasonal split is reported, not blended.
2. **Doc-collection turnaround** — request sent → items complete, in days, from thread timestamps, for chases the fleet drafted vs. the partner's stated before-baseline. This is the CPA-native headline metric; it exists only if week-1 capture starts it (runbook Delta 5).
3. **Approval rate** — approved ÷ (approved + rejected), reported even when unflattering; it's the number that proves a person at the firm holds the pen.

**Client-satisfaction signal (soft, never fabricated into a metric):** if clients spontaneously remark on responsiveness, the partner's *retelling* of that is a quotable moment with consent — we do not survey the firm's clients, ever; they are not our customers and contacting them would violate the engagement boundary.
